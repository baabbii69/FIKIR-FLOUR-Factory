Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Grain and Precision.

Founded in 1962, FIKIR FLOUR Factory has grown from a regional grain processor into one of East Africa's most trusted flour manufacturing companies. Our modern facilities combine the rigorous quality standards of traditional milling with state-of-the-art automation and food science.

We supply industrial bakeries, artisan producers, food service distributors, and retail chains — all from our ISO-certified production campus in Adama.
