GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM :
Design system for "Fikir Flour Factory" — a premium industrial flour manufacturing company based in Ethiopia. 

BRAND IDENTITY:
- Company name: Fikir Flour Factory
- Tagline: "From Grain to Greatness"
- Tone: Premium, trustworthy, industrial yet warm — like a luxury food brand meets a serious B2B manufacturer

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Display / Headings: Cormorant Garamond or Freight Display — elegant serif with weight contrast
- Body / UI: DM Sans — clean, modern, highly legible
- Accent labels: DM Mono — small uppercase tracking for tags, labels, stats

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary Background: #FFFFFF
- Secondary Background: #F7F3EC (warm off-white, parchment feel)
- Dark Section Background: #1C1308 (near-black warm brown)
- Primary Accent: #C49A3C (rich harvest gold)
- Secondary Accent: #8B5E2A (deep amber brown)
- Green Accent: #3A5C3B (muted forest green — for sustainability/certification badges)
- Text Dark: #1C1308
- Text Medium: #5A4A36
- Text Light: #9C8E7E
- Border Color: #E2D9CC
- White: #FFFFFF

SPACING: Generous whitespace. Minimum 80px section padding vertical. Content max-width 1280px centered.

IMAGERY STYLE: Real photography — golden wheat fields, industrial mill machinery, flour in motion, grain close-ups, workers in factory, aerial shots of silos. Never illustrations or icons as primary visuals.

OVERALL FEEL: Think Puratos, Cargill, or Meneba flour company websites — serious industrial authority combined with warmth of grain and bread. Clean grid. No clutter.
