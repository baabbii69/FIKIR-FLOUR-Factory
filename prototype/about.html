About FIKIR FLOUR Factory

Our Mission

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Quality.

FIKIR FLOUR Factory exists to be the most trusted flour manufacturing partner in the food industry. Our mission is simple: deliver consistent, premium-quality flour that our clients can build their business on — backed by transparent practices, food safety excellence, and an unwavering commitment to partnership.

From our first stone mill in 1962 to today's fully automated, ISO-certified production campus, this mission has never changed.

Our Core Values

Quality Without Compromise

Every bag of flour that leaves our facility has passed through our 14-stage quality control process. We set the standard — for ourselves, and for the industry.

Partnership First

We don't see our clients as customers — we see them as long-term partners. Our commercial team works alongside your procurement and production teams as a genuine extension of your business.

Since 1962

Our History

From a single stone mill on the Awash River to a 48-acre production campus — six decades of growth, investment, and unwavering commitment to quality.

1962

Founded by Harold FIKIR FLOUR Factory

Harold FIKIR FLOUR Factory establishes a small grain processing facility on the banks of the Awash River with a team of 18 employees and a single stone mill.

1978

First Industrial Bakery Contract

FIKIR FLOUR Factory signs its first major supply contract with Midwest Bread Co., marking the transition from regional processor to industrial flour supplier.

1995

Modernisation & ISO Certification

A $12M modernisation project installs automated milling lines and in-line quality systems. FIKIR FLOUR Factory achieves its first ISO food safety certification.

2008

International Export Launch

First international shipments to Mexico and Canada. Within five years, FIKIR FLOUR Factory is exporting to 14 countries across three continents.

2019

R&D Centre of Excellence Opens

A dedicated 12,000 sq ft food science facility opens, housing grain analysis labs, pilot milling lines, and a full test bakery.

2024

Phase III Expansion Complete

$42M expansion brings daily capacity to 2,400 MT. FIKIR FLOUR Factory now supplies 850+ clients across 38 countries with 480 full-time employees.
